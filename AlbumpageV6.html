Siren Songs of the Counter Culture

Rise Against

Release Date: August 10, 2004

The Album Siren Songs of the Counter Culture was released in August of 2004
by the band Rise Against. This was their third studio album and was their first album
with a major studio. The album featured songs such as Swing Life Away, Give it All, and
Life Less Frieghtening.
